# Chandigarh & Mohali Tech Recruitment Database (Verified)
**Compiled July 2026 · Panchkula excluded per scope**

## Methodology & honesty notes

This database was built with live web search (LinkedIn snippets, company career pages, Naukri/Indeed/Glassdoor/Internshala listings, Tracxn/press coverage) — not from memory. Ground rules that shaped every row:

- **No fabricated emails or phone numbers.** Where a field says "Not publicly available," that means no verbatim public source was found — it is not a placeholder for a guess.
- **No invented names or LinkedIn URLs.** Every HR/founder contact listed has a real, search-verified LinkedIn profile and at least one piece of evidence tying them to that company (a hiring post, a job listing, a press mention).
- Several commonly-assumed Chandigarh/Mohali companies were **checked and excluded** because no real local office could be verified (e.g., Kwalee, Xebia, Sopra Steria, Cavisson Systems, GlobalLogic, Cognizant IT-park presence, Coding Ninjas dedicated office) — see the full agent notes if you want the exclusion reasoning.
- Company size, "hiring freshers," and "last hiring evidence" are all sourced inline; where sources conflicted (e.g. employee counts), the most specific/recent source is cited.
- **27 named HR/recruiter/founder contacts** were verified across 55 unique companies. That's the honest ceiling of what public search can surface — most companies, especially large MNCs with centralized/campus hiring, don't expose an individual recruiter's name publicly. Treat the "location unverified" / "role unclear" flags on a few contacts as real caveats, not filler.

Full machine-readable version: `chandigarh_mohali_tech_hr_database.csv` (62 rows incl. multi-contact companies, Excel-ready).
